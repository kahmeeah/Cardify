About Cardify

Cardify is a tool that analyzes your Spotify listening habits to create a personalized infographic in the form of a fake ID Card.

Getting started is easy! Simply login with your Spotify account, and our app will securely retrieve your listening history. Once the data is fetched, you'll get access to your personalized infographic that reflects your unique musical journey.
