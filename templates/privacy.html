Privacy Policy

Cardify prioritizes the protection and privacy of your personal information. Rest assured, Cardify does not share any data with third parties. By using Cardify, you agree to a temporary and limited usage of your Spotify account name and data solely for the purpose of generating your personalized infographic.

Data Collection and Usage: Cardify does not collect or retain any of your data. The information accessed from your Spotify account is used exclusively to create the infographic and is not stored beyond the duration of your session.

Revoking Permissions: If you wish to revoke Cardify's access to your Spotify account, you can do so easily by visiting your Apps page on Spotify. Locate Cardify and click on "REMOVE ACCESS." For a more detailed guide on this process, please refer to Spotify's official instructions.
